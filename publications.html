FEATURED RESEARCH

"A Study of Enhancing Thermal Comfort and Development of an Intelligent Efficient Cooling System for Parked Car Interiors"

9th BSME International Conference on Thermal Engineering
Presenter • December 19-21, 2024 • Dhaka, Bangladesh

This research addresses the critical issue of thermal discomfort in parked vehicles by developing an intelligent cooling system that enhances passenger comfort while optimizing energy efficiency. The study combines experimental validation with comprehensive CFD analysis to create a practical solution for automotive thermal management.
